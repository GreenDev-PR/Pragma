Frequently Asked Questions

Who is GreenDev?
We are a technology company focused on providing agriculture solutions in the Caribbean region.

What is Pragma?
Pragma is our irrigation management solution.

How are irrigation requirements calculated?
Irrigation requirements calculations are based on various hydro-climate variables that are obtained and/or estimated for your geographic location. The volume of irrigation directly depends on a crop’s evapotranspiration, rainfall and the crop’s growth stage. For more information click here.

What advantages does it have for a farmer?
A farmer will be able to effectively manage a crops growth using your farms geographical location and exploiting satellite and radar data.

What type of data is available for researchers?
Because Pragma records hydro-climate data of Puerto Rico, Pragma provides access to such data for research purposes to registered scientists.

Who is behind this scientific method in Puerto Rico?
In Puerto Rico, there is substantial amount of research regarding crop’s water requirement. Dr. Eric Harmsen from the Agricultural Engineering Department at UPRM is a lead researcher in the field and has contributed to the development of the GOES-PRWEB algorithm that powers Pragma.
